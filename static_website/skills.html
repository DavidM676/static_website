How to do a wheelie on a pedal bike:

  1. Put you bike seat all the way down. This will give you a lower center of gravity

  2. Start to ride at a slow pace

  3. Position the pedals so that your dominant foot is at the 2 o'clock position

  4. Stand up on the bike

  5. Push down on the pedals and at the same time pull back

  6. Once you get the bike up, sit back down

  7. If the bike goes to far back hit the brakes, if the bike is to far forwards pedal

  8. Keep practicing and eventually you will find the balance point
